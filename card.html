Mexican Redknee Tarantula

  • Scientific Name: Brachypelma smithi
  • Average Length: 10 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 30yrs(F) / 5yrs(M)
  • Habitat: Deciduous tropical forests

The mature B. smithi has a dark-colored body with orange patches on the joints of its legs. The second element of the legs is orange-red.

Following molting, the colors are more pronounced. The dark portion is very black, while the orange-red portions are far more on the reddish side.
